System

Cut, not branded

Every piece is cut from the same pattern language — the print is the piece, the label stays inside. No face, no name; the amber signal is the only voice the system has. Inside, each garment carries a woven plate with its number in the edition.

  • 01KARST— camo set
  • 02BOULDER— zip hoodie
  • 03RAVINE— flare pants
  • 04FLINT— boxy tee

Design

Street pieces, cut like gear

Obsessive, precise patterns — every seam placed with intent, until only purpose remains. Designed under the volcanoes of central México: the mountain sets the standard, the street is where the edition lives. If a piece fails, it never enters the run.
